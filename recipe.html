Malaysain Rendang Curry

Ingredients

Main

Ingredient Quantity
Stewing Beef (diced) 450g
Red Onions 2x medium
Garlic 4x fat gloves
Ginger thumb
Lime 1x
Coconut Milk 1 Can / 400ml
Jasmine Rice 200g
Soy Sauce 1 tbsp
Veg Stock 1 cube

Rendang Spice Blend

Spice Amount
Coriander Seed 2 tbs
Cumin Seeds 1 tbs
Fennel Seeds 3/4 tbs
Chilli Powder 1/2 tbs
Ground Tumeric 1/2 tsp
Whole Cloves 1/4 tsp
Ground Cinnamon 1/4 tsp
Cardamom pods 1/2 tsp
Pepper 1/4 tsp

Recipe

Pre-Cooking Prep

  1. Zest and juice lime
  2. Roughly chop onions, garlic, ginger
  3. Dice beef
  4. Open coconut milk
  5. Soak rice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add onions, ginger, garlic and spices to the blender with 75ml water to create smooth spice paste.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil and fry spice paste gently for 5 mins.
  3. Add beef and stir-fry for 5 mins.
  4. Add coconut milk, soy sauce, lime zest and juice, optional sweetener
  5. add 150ml water and 1 tsp salt.
  6. Stir and bring to gentle simmer, leave uncovered for 2.5 Hours

Serve with Nann bread and Lemon Rice
